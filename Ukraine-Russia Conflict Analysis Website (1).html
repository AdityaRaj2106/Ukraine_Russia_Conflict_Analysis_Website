Reasons for the war

There are quite a few reasons for the war.

  • The relations between Russia and Ukraine have been bad since a long time.
  • In 1991, Ukraine declared itself independent from the Soviet Union.
  • The broken Soviet Union was now called Russia.
  • NATO was an organization that prevented Russia from annexing European countries.
  • However, Ukraine was not a member of NATO. It wanted to become its member.
  • Russia did not want that to happen. Before Ukraine could join NATO, Russia invaded Ukraine on 22 February 2022.

Results of the war

The impact of the war has been overall bad. The major consequences are given below.

  • Thousands of Europeans have lost their lives already.
  • The danger of the third World War is on the world if powerful countries like the U.S.A, China, e.t.c., jump into the war directly.
  • The climate of Europe has also been greatly affected, giving global warming a boost.
  • Many countries have imposed certain bans on Russia.
  • Russia's economy has been greatly affected as many countries have reduced trading with Russia.

India has also been impacted.

  • Some Indians stuck in Ukraine lost their lives in the early phase of the war.
  • The prices of crude oil in India have gone up as Russia is unable to supply it.

Who is right?

If looked at correctly, no one is right in the war. Russia attacked Ukraine knowing that Ukraine is harmless for them. Some people believe that #RussiaIsRight while others support #StandWithUkraine. However, India is completely in the favour of the third, i.e., #PeaceWithAll. It is necessary for the war to stop to reinforce peace.

Can the war be stopped?

There are some unlikely solutions to stop the war.

  • The easiest solution is to negotiate. However, it seems completely impossible that the two countrues would negotiate after more than a year of war.
  • The interference of an international organization like the UN might be useful, but the UN is unable to do so and seems to have lost its significance.

"No more war, let diplomacy take charge."
